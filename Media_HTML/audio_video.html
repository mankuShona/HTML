<audio> element

Basic Syntax:

        
            <audio controls autoplay loop>
            <source src="audio.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
                Your browser does not support the audio element.
            </audio>
  1. controls attribute: Adds audio playback controls (play, pause, volume, etc.).
  2. <source> tag: Specifies the audio file and its format.
  3. autoplay: Automatically starts playing the media.
  4. loop: Repeats the media playback.

Example:

<video> element

        
            <video width="320" height="240" controls poster="poster.jpeg">
            <source src="video.mp4" type="video/mp4">

            </video>
