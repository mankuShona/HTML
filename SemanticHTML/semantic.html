The Power of Semantic HTML

Making a semantically correct HTML Page is considered as one of the Healtiest Practices

Introduction

Semantic HTML is crucial for creating accessible, SEO-friendly, and maintainable websites.

Why Use Semantic HTML?

  • Improved accessibility
  • Better SEO
  • Enhanced user experience

Best Practices

  1. Use elements for their intended purpose.
  2. Nest elements logically.Ensure that elements are nested correctly to reflect the hierarchical structure of your content.
  3. Validate your HTML. Use a validator to check your HTML for errors and ensure it adheres to web standards.
